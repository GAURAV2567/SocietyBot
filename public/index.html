Common Questions

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know about SocietyBot pricing

Can I switch from SaaS to Perpetual License later?
Yes. If you're on a SaaS plan and decide to purchase a Perpetual License, we'll credit up to 3 months of paid SaaS fees toward the license price.
What payment gateway do residents use?
Razorpay — supporting UPI, Cards, Net Banking, and Wallets. For SaaS plans, we use a shared Razorpay account. On Starter+, you connect your own Razorpay account so payments go directly to your society.
Do residents need to download any app?
No. Everything runs on WhatsApp — which residents already have. No new apps, no logins, no confusion. Adoption is instant.
What happens after the 6/12-month update period on Perpetual?
Your bot keeps working — you just won't receive new features or patches. You can subscribe to our Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) for ₹4,999–9,999/yr to stay updated, or self-maintain the code.
Can the Agency License be used for our own internal societies?
Absolutely. The Agency License covers unlimited deployments — whether for your own projects, client deployments, or both. White-label and resell rights are included.
How long does setup take?
Approximately 30 minutes from signing up to a live WhatsApp bot. We provide a guided onboarding call for all paid plans and handle the technical setup end-to-end.
